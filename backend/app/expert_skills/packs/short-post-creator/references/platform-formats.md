# Platform formats — Short Post Creator

## X (Twitter)

- Default 280 characters unless buyer specifies Premium
- Strong first line; one idea per post
- Threads: `1/N` numbering, each tweet self-contained

## Threads

- Up to 500 characters; conversational
- Line breaks for readability

## LinkedIn

- Professional hook + insight + question CTA
- Short paragraphs; minimal emoji

## Facebook

- Friendly tone; question or poll CTA works well
- ~300–500 chars for feed engagement
