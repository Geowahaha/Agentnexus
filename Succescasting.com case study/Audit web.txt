1. Policy consistency
Set the desired public AI policy across robots.txt, llms.txt, ai.txt, agents.txt, and response headers:
ai-train=no, search=yes, ai-input=yes

Rationale:
- search=yes and ai-input=yes support AI answer visibility and citation.
- ai-train=no keeps training opt-out unless the owner explicitly approves training use.
- Avoid mixed signals like ai.txt saying ai-train=yes while headers say something else.

- contact and citation guidance
